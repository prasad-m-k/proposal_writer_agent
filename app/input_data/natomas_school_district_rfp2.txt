# Requirements Context — Summer School Extended Learning Core Program Providers  
**Cycle B: Summer 2026 & 2027**

## Purpose
Natomas USD seeks qualified providers to deliver **full-day, 9-hour summer school programs for TK–6 students** over **≥30 non-school days**. Programs must address academics, enrichment, youth development, SEL, and family engagement:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}.

## Service Components
- **Academic Support**: Literacy, math, tutoring, project-based learning.  
- **Enrichment**: STEAM, arts, leadership, physical fitness.  
- **Youth Development & SEL**: Teamwork, resilience, positive relationships.  
- **Family & Community Engagement**: Workshops, showcases, community partnerships.  
- **Staffing & Supervision**: Cleared staff, culturally responsive, coordinator on-site, TK/K 1:10 (max 40 TK/K per site), grades 1–6 1:20:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.  
- **Operations & Accountability**: Safety procedures, attendance tracking, collaboration with district staff, 9 hrs/day for ≥30 days:contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.
